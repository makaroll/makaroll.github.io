This Privacy Policy outlines how Studio Maka LLC ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the online feature of _Asterika: Phantom Rose Refrain_ (the "Game"). By enabling the online feature of the Game, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

The company responsible for your information is:

- Contact Email: studiomakadev+support@gmail.com

#### 3. Information We Collect and Why

We collect a minimal amount of information to provide and improve the Game's features.

Information You Provide:

- Steam ID: When you use online features, we collect your public Steam ID. This is used solely to operate features such as online leaderboards for high scores and to attribute ownership to custom stages you create and share using the built-in beatmap editor.

Information Collected Automatically:

- Anonymous Analytics: We collect non-personally identifiable data, such as gameplay events and feature usage statistics (e.g., "click counters"). This anonymous data helps us understand how players interact with the Game so we can improve the experience for everyone.

#### 4. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services to operate the Game's features. These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

- Steam: The Game is distributed on Steam, and your use of the Steam platform is subject to Valve's Privacy Policy.
- Supabase: We use Supabase for backend services, including storing leaderboard data and user-created beatmaps.
- Firebase: We use Google's Firebase service for collecting the anonymous gameplay analytics mentioned above.

#### 5. Your Rights and Data Deletion

We respect your right to control your personal data.

Data Deletion: You can permanently and immediately delete your personal data (your Steam ID and associated leaderboard scores) from our servers at any time. This action can be performed directly through the in-game settings menu.

#### 6. Children's Privacy

The Game is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information from our servers.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the Game or on our official website.
