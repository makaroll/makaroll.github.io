## I need help with restoring In App Purchases

### **Android:**

Purchases are tied to your Google Account and can be restored on any Android device using that same account.

### **Checklist:**

1. Ensure the Play Store is set to the exact Google account used for the purchase. If you have multiple accounts, the correct one must be the active default.
2. Download the latest version of the app from the Play Store.
3. Touch and hold the Google Play Store app.
4. Go to your device Settings > Apps. Find the **Google Play Store** > Storage > tap **Clear Cache**. Repeat this exact step for the game app. (Some devices might have different label but you need to delete "Cache". Please refer to your device's manual if menu can't be found.)
5. Restart your device, open the game, and try restoring again.
