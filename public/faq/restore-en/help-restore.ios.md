## I need help with restoring In App Purchases

### **iOS:**

Purchases are tied to your Apple ID and can be restored on any iOS device using that same account.

### **Checklist:**

1. Check your App Store purchase history to ensure you are logged into the exact Apple ID used to make the purchase.
2. Download the latest version of the app from the App Store.
3. Restart your device, open the game, and try restoring again.
